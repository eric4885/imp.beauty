About IMP Beauty

IMP Beauty is an engaging online Spider Solitaire game, faithfully inspired by the Microsoft classic. Featuring 4 suits, it provides both challenge and entertainment for card game enthusiasts. Whether you are a casual player or a solitaire veteran, IMP Beauty offers a sleek, Apple-inspired design and smooth gameplay across all devices.

How to Play

The goal of Spider Solitaire is to arrange all cards into sequences of the same suit from King down to Ace. Move cards between columns, uncover hidden cards, and build complete suits to clear them from the board. IMP Beauty follows the classic rules but uses all four suits for maximum challenge.
