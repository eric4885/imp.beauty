How to Master Spider Solitaire

1

Build descending sequences from King to Ace in the same suit to complete a full set

2

Move cards between columns strategically to uncover hidden cards underneath

3

Use the stock pile wisely when you run out of moves - timing is crucial

4

Plan ahead and think several moves in advance to avoid getting stuck

5

Clear all eight complete sequences to win and become a Spider Solitaire champion

6

Practice regularly to improve your strategy and achieve better completion times
