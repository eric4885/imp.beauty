About Imp Beauty Spider Solitaire

Imp Beauty recreates the original Microsoft Spider Solitaire down to every animation and shortcut. Choose 1-suit (Easy), 2-suit (Medium) or 4-suit (Hard) and clear the table by building K-to-A sequences in the same suit. Completed runs are automatically removed—just like Windows.

How to Play

Click to move cards, right-click for quick send-to-stack. Empty columns are gold—use them to rearrange long sequences. When you're stuck, deal a new row from the stock (bottom-right). Clear all eight full runs to win.

Pro Tips

Prioritise emptying at least one column early—it becomes a free parking spot for any card. When two same-suit sequences overlap, try to merge them into one long run before dealing new rows; fewer face-down cards mean fewer surprises.
